# COURSE: Tư tưởng Hồ Chí Minh (BAA00003)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00003
- **English Name**: Ho Chi Minh’s Ideology
- **Vietnamese Name**: Tư tưởng Hồ Chí Minh
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Ngô Tuấn Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 8
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 25 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 57.5 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to the Subject
- Chapter 1: Concepts, Subjects, Research Methods, and the Importance of Studying Ho Chi Minh's Thought
- Chapter 2: Foundations, Formation Process, and Development of Ho Chi Minh's Thought
- Chapter 3: Ho Chi Minh's Thought on National Independence Associated with Socialism
- Chapter 4: Ho Chi Minh's Thought on the Communist Party of Vietnam and the State of the People, By the People, and For the People
- Chapter 5: Ho Chi Minh's Thought on National Unity and International Solidarity
- Chapter 6: Ho Chi Minh's Thought on Culture, Ethics, and Humanity

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Present the concept of Ho Chi Minh's thought and the basic ideas, arguments, and viewpoints that emerged during the process of forming and developing Ho Chi Minh's Thought. Learners can analyze and explain the fundamental content, thoughts, and viewpoints of Ho Chi Minh's Thought. Apply the values of Ho Chi Minh's Thought in their lives. Develop and enhance the skills of analysis, synthesis, and systematization of the knowledge that learners have acquired
- G2.Apply the values of Ho Chi Minh's Thought in their lives. Develop and enhance the skills of analysis, synthesis, and systematization of the knowledge that learners have acquired
- G3.Develop and enhance learners' skills in commenting, evaluating, and comparing the content they have studied for practical application.
